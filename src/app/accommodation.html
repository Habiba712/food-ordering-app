Night 1 - 2: Reykjavik

Reykjavik Marina – Berjaya Iceland Hotels

CMýrargata 2, 101 Reykjavík, Iceland

Location Perfect for business travelers as well as families, this urban hotel is located about aquarter mile from tourist attractions and the center of Reykjavik. The nearest shops can bereached within about a quarter mile. Facilities The hostel has 108 non-smoking rooms, including 2 single rooms and 84 double rooms, whichare located on 4 stories and are reachable by elevator. English-speaking staff at thereception desk in the lobby are ready to assist you with check-in and check-out. Amenitiesinclude a baggage storage service, safe, ATM and drinks machine. Wireless internet access(for a fee) allows guests to stay connected while on vacation. The tour desk offersassistance with booking excursions. The hostel offers various facilities for guests withdisabilities. Wheelchair-accessible facilities are available. A fireplace creates a cozyatmosphere. Film fans will find plenty of 'lights, camera, action' at the cinema. There arealso a number of shops that make for great strolling and browsing. Additional amenitiesinclude a newspaper stand, a TV room and a library. Guests arriving by car can park theirvehicles in the parking lot for a fee. Further services and facilities include a 24-hoursecurity service, a car rental service, room service, a laundry service, a hairdresser, acoin-operated laundry and a hotel shuttle bus. The business center is on hand for guests'business requirements and provides a fax machine.
